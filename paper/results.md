# Results

The analysis of monsoon rainfall from 1980–2020 shows significant inter-annual variability. The long-term average rainfall was 869.905 mm with a standard deviation of 78.189 and coefficient of variation of 8.988%, indicating moderate variability.

Rainfall anomaly analysis identified several drought and flood years. The year 2002 recorded the highest negative anomaly, indicating severe drought conditions, while 1988 showed the highest positive anomaly, indicating excessive rainfall.

Agricultural productivity data shows a strong long-term increase in both rice and wheat yields. Rice yield increased from approximately 2000 kg/ha to 4009 kg/ha, while wheat increased from 1630 kg/ha to 3440 kg/ha.

However, productivity dips were observed during drought years, showing sensitivity to rainfall variability.
